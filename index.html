⚡ Featured — Solar-Powered IoT
EcoSmart Water Station

An off-grid, solar-powered water dispensing station built for community use. An ESP32 tracks drum level with an ultrasonic sensor and doses chlorine automatically, adjusting pump duration so every dose stays consistent from full to nearly empty. A coin slot and toggle switch handle pay-per-use access to filtered water or a 10-minute device-charging session, with a free emergency override that bypasses payment entirely. Power comes from a 60W solar panel and micro water turbines charging a 12V LiFePO4 battery, and a companion web app gives live level, chlorine estimate, pump status, and remote dosing controls from any device.
